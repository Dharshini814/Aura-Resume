Is AURA Resume actually free?
Yes! You can build, optimize, and export standard ATS-friendly resumes completely for free. We offer a premium tier for advanced AI credits and exclusive executive templates, but the core engine remains free forever.
How does the ATS Scanner work?
Our scanner uses the same parsing logic (Taleo, Workday, Greenhouse) that top companies use. It extracts your text, scores the structural integrity, checks for keyword density against your target job title, and flags formatting that might break the system.
Does it work for non-tech roles?
Absolutely. While heavily loved by software engineers and data scientists, AURA's AI is trained on millions of successful resumes across Finance, Healthcare, Marketing, and Operations. The principles of a machine-readable resume apply universally.
Is my data secure?
Security is a top priority. Your personal information is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your resume data to third parties, and you can permanently delete your account and associated documents with one click.
